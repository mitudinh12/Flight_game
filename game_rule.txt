
Your initial co2 budget is 10 000 units. Every flight will consume your co2 budget

If you travel to a country in the same continent, you will use 1000 units from your co2 budget

If you travel to a country in a different continent, you will use 3000 units from your co2 budget

You cannot flight to the same country twice.

You get 1 point for every successful trip. The game ends when you use up your co2 budget.

Now, let the game begin. Travel to as many countries as possible and be the first in the leaderboard!
